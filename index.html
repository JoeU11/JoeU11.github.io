About me

Hi! I'm Joe and I'm a current Computer Science Student at Oregon State University. I got my start in programming through the Actualize bootcamp and have been learning ever since. Prior to coding, I worked in the legal/business field as a paralegal for Kirkland & Ellis in NYC and then as a licensing specialist for Esri, Inc. My first exposure to software development came through working with the developers at Esri, and it wasn't long before I realized I wanted to do this for a living. I'm still fairly early in my tech journey, but I can't wait to see where this road takes me.

When I'm not at my desk, you can find me playing/watching soccer (especially Real Madrid!) or hanging out with my dogs. Also known to get wrapped up in a good video game or book.

WePlay Pickup Sports - Capstone Project

An app allowing users to see, schedule and sign up for pickup sports events.

Highlights:

  • Ruby on Rails backend || Vue.js frontend
  • Integrates with Google Calendar API using OAuth
  • Calculates expected participants based on geographic distance and user-selected preferred times of playing
  • All times display locally

About me

Hi! I'm Joe and I write code. I got my start in coding through the Actualize bootcamp (such an amazing experience) and have been learning ever since. Prior to coding, I worked in the legal/business field as a paralegal for Kirkland & Ellis in NYC and then as a licensing specialist for Esri, Inc. which is where I developed the interest in software development that led me to change careers. I'm still fairly early in my tech journey but I can't wait to see where this road takes me.

When I'm not at my desk, you can find me playing or watching soccer (hala Madrid!) or hanging out with my dogs. Also known to get wrapped up in a good video game or book.

WePlay Pickup Sports - Capstone Project

An app allowing users to see, schedule and sign up for pickup sports events.

Highlights:

  • Ruby on Rails backend || Vue.js frontend
  • Integrates with Google Calendar API using OAuth to add events to user's calendar
  • Calculates expected participants based on geographic distance and user-selected preferred times of playing
  • All times display locally
